HPC Game

Instructions: Drag blocks from the pile at the bottom into one of the 3 possible HPC resources. Blocks will light up if they are accepted by the scheduler! Blocks must fit completely inside the resource based on CPUs, walltime, and memory requested. Stats will update in the table on the right as you complete the puzzle. Optimize using your best judgement - what is the most important factor to consider, in your opinion? Keep in mind that one small block inside each of the 3 HPC resources equals 1 hour.
